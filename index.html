3D SCULPTING

3D sculpting is a digital art form that involves shaping and modeling virtual objects in a three-dimensional space, much like traditional clay sculpting. Using a software such as Blender, artists can manipulate digital "clay" with brushes and tools to create detailed characters, objects, and environments.
